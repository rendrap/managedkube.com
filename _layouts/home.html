k8sBot is a next-gen virtual assistant that will help you troubleshoot your applications running on k8s clusters.

Our agent runs in your k8s cluster to gather information from the multiple Kubernetes resources necessary to diagnose a particular issue. We pull out the relevant nuggets of information so you don't have to sift through lines and lines of kubectl cli output.

What does k8sBot do?

K8sBot makes Kubernetes easier to use. We know it’s complex and you need to gather multiple Kubernetes resources to diagnose issues. For example, to get an ingress fully working, you have to first understand how it links to a service and a pod.


K8sBot is for infrastructure engineers and for developers who aren’t focused on Kubernetes every day. We’ll help you avoid frustrating troubleshooting time and find those configuration issues instantaneously.

K8sBot instantly pulls the troubleshooting information you need from your clusters and extracts the nugget of information that you're looking for. You will:

Save troubleshooting time by gathering and analyzing relevant Kubernetes resources faster

Help novice users navigate the complexity of Kubernetes clusters

Facilitate team-driven troubleshooting discussions by bringing Kubernetes information into Slack's collaborative environment

How it Works

Ask k8sBot a question and it will go to your cluster to get the pertinent information and display it back to you in easy-to-read sentences.
