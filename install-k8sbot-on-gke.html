Install ManagedKube's k8sBot from GKE’s Marketplace

Installing ManagedKube's k8sBot tool on GKE is as easy as one click - see below for installation instructions.

1

Search for and click on ‘k8sBot’ in the GKE marketplace or follow this link

2

Click ‘Configure’ on the ManagedKube k8sBot application page

3

Click ‘Subscribe'. Wait for subscription to activate

4

Click on 'Register with ManagedKube'

5

On the installation instructions page, click 'Add to Slack'

6

On the Slack App page, choose the channel to install k8sBot to and click Authorize

7

Invite k8sBot to your channel and ask for help to start the set-up process ('@k8sbot help')

8

Ask k8sBot to generate an agent config and turn it on in your cluster with 'kubectl apply -f '
